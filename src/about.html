Studio Apartment is an independent design studio specializing in brand identity and interface design.

We help people say what they’re trying to say, better. We do this by combining user-centered functional design principles with the visual arts. Our global headquarters are located in New York City.*

* In my apartment. (Oh hi, my name is Gage Salzano, the Founder and Creative Director of Studio Apartment.)
